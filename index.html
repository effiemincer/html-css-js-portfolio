Torah Verses

Pesukim for Your Name

There is a custom to recite a verse from Tanach before the final Yihyu L'ratzon at the end of Shemoneh Esrei. You can recite a verse whose first and last letters match the first and last letters of your name, or one that contains your name entirely — both are perfectly fine options (heard from HaRav Yitzchak Berkowitz שליט״א). When a verse matches both criteria, I call it a Super Match!

Two-part name? Join with a dash (like בן-ציון). Have more than one name? Separate them with a space.
